Our Services

Basic plan

Our basic maintenance plan includes an oil change and tire rotation for $50. This is ideal for customers who need routine maintenance and want to ensure their car is running smoothly.

Premium plan

Our premium maintenance plan includes a comprehensive maintenance package for $150. This includes an oil change, tire rotation, fluid inspection, brake inspection, and a full vehicle inspection. Our expert mechanics will provide you with a detailed report on your car's condition and any recommended repairs or upgrades.

MAINTANANCE

Basic plan

Our basic customization plan includes the installation of aftermarket parts and accessories for a flat fee of $200. This is ideal for customers who want to personalize their car with upgrades such as a new sound system or custom rims.

Premium plan

Our premium customization plan includes a customized car build starting at $10,000. This includes a consultation with our expert customizers to discuss your specific needs and preferences, a customized design, installation of premium parts and accessories, and a 1-year warranty on all parts and labor. We will work closely with you to ensure that your customized car meets your exact specifications and exceeds your expectations.

CUSTOMIZATION

Basic plan

We will buy your car at market value, based on factors such as condition, make, and model.

Premium plan

We will buy your car at 5% above the market value. We will also conduct a free vehicle inspection to ensure the car is in good condition, and provide you with an immediate payment upon acceptance of our offer.
